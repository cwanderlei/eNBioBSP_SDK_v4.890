=============================
  eNBSP SDK license policy
=============================

eNBSP SDK is composed of BSP module and high-speed 1:N matching module 
(eNSearch) for fingerprint application software development and has its 
additional license policy.

1. BSP
   1) License management method
      Accessible by entering BSP installation serial number.
   2) License policy
      Users are given a license number when purchasing SDK.

2. eNSearch
   1) License management method
      Accessible by installing an additional license file.
   2) License policy
      Users are required to purchase license for individual PC. 
      (Free for less than 5,000 fingers[Registered version]/1,000 fingers[Evaluation version]).
